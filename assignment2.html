Design Alternatives:

According to the tasks of Project Vivy, a home sense is necessary for user and Vivy to interact. User could enter the home sense through same special instructions. We require that users should in a safe state (e.g. lying on the chair at home). Two different style of the home sense is considered during designing.

The first home sense is in realistic one. It seemed that users are transported into another world as the real world. Users could be more immersed in the world and actively interact with Vivy. But the crucial drawback is, user may not be able to distinguish between real world and the home sense. It could result in chaos. Therefore the design is aborted.

The second home sense is an immersive one like the one descriped in SAO.

Vivy works in a virtual environment which is exactly different from the robots in the current period. Interactive intelligent robots like Siri are installed on your Mobil phone while Vivy directly interact with your brain.

We suppose a bright future where the techniques of brain computer interaction has developed well while it is not allowed to write in brain. In this future, most of the people plant brain computer interaction devices in there brain and these devices can connect the online computers through wires or wifi. For better official supervision, high intelligent brain interaction AI usage like Vivy can only applied with wires connected.

To install Vivy, you need to fill a form about your personal information and sign on several protocols. After mental assessment and rigorous verification, you will have access to Vivy through wire connection at home.



Overall design:

Guidance system

The most important part of the system. In this process, Vivy do the following things:
  • Check the surrounding environment and keep the checking process on during usage for both mental and physical safety. It will automatically quit when emergencies happen and can also force return through pressing physical button.
  • The virtual environment will be archived when you are in the virtual environment.
  • You can choose mode of the environment. For example, your virtual environment may like Minecraft or animates. Real world environment is not allowed because it may add chaos to your memory. Moreover, the Vivy will add some details against common senses which you firmly believe in order to emphasis that it is unreal.
  • You can choose the archives in the guidance system just like choosing the OS in the bat system in today’s computors. The AI bots and the environment are archived separately for more freedom.


Env system

  • Constructed according to the mode you choose and the keynote you desire(read from brain while not ). You may choose to be in a classroom or your favourite game MineCraft.


AI system

  • There are two AI servers in the world the first is the world constructor and the second one is Vivy
  • The AI Vivy can construct it’s own way of speaking and thinking on the base of your desire. But Vivy will not imitate appearance because it cause discomfort

UI system

  • A customer friendly holonomic projecting screen which includes option and quit button. It appears where you want it to be when you call it.



Storyboard:

Act.1

Title: playing music

Vivy is not visible to others as a personal assistant. It can be designed only interact in mind but this might make specific group have trouble using Vivy or make some people feel strange, so it still able to communicate with user in voice.

Act.2

Title: The old man's first time using Vivy

With powerful NLP model, all interaction can done without computer knowledge. This is special useful for old men who have trouble in learning how to use electronic device.

Act.3

Title: No game

Vivy can also be used in restricted mode to prevent potential abuse in adolescents.

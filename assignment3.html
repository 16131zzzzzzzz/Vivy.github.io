User Evaluation

CUBE / 2021-7-25 / Assignment3

User testing and evaluation

Task 1 Inner voice communication

  • After successfully installed Project Vivy, tried to wake up Vivy through your mind.
  • Try to “say” hello to Vivy and received a positive response from Vivy.
  • Try to shut Vivy down, then called Vivy again to ensure that Vivy is offline.

Task 2 In-mind friend & Individuated personality

  • After activate Vivy, try to communicate with her as human-beings do.
  • For example, sharing your own experience today with Vivy or discuss the news to ensure that Vivy could interact with you as real person with NLP model.
  • Change the personality of Vivy to make her a cool girl or loli, then interact with her to see if the style of Vivy’s communication is changed.

Task 3 Multi-dimensional interaction & Sense Enhancement

  • Try multimedia functions e.g. music player ore health detector in-mind.
  • Try sense enhancement in various circumstances e.g. driving, working.

CUBE / 2021-7-11 / Assignment1

Problem discription:

With the modernized society, more and more people have no time or are unwilling to have social contact, which resulting in the common loneliness among modern people. Most people are overwhelmed by the fast-pace world without time to take a breath. Therefore, those people need someone to accompany and assistant, such as a chat bot.

What’s more, traditional bot may not fully satisfied the request of people. For those robots have real entity, they are less like human in appearance and can never accompany a person for 24-hour time. For those robots are only virtual, interacting may be complex and not direct. A new approach of human-machine communication is expected.

CUBE / 2021-7-18 / Assignment2

Design Alternatives:

According to the tasks of Project Vivy, a home sense is necessary for user and Vivy to interact. User could enter the home sense through same special instructions. We require that users should in a safe state (e.g. lying on the chair at home). Two different style of the home sense is considered during designing.

The first home sense is in realistic one. It seemed that users are transported into another world as the real world. Users could be more immersed in the world and actively interact with Vivy. But the crucial drawback is, user may not be able to distinguish between real world and the home sense. It could result in chaos. Therefore the design is aborted.

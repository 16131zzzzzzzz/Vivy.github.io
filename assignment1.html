Problem discription:

With the modernized society, more and more people have no time or are unwilling to have social contact, which resulting in the common loneliness among modern people. Most people are overwhelmed by the fast-pace world without time to take a breath. Therefore, those people need someone to accompany and assistant, such as a chat bot.

What’s more, traditional bot may not fully satisfied the request of people. For those robots have real entity, they are less like human in appearance and can never accompany a person for 24-hour time. For those robots are only virtual, interacting may be complex and not direct. A new approach of human-machine communication is expected.

In addition, modern people are constrained by overwhelming technical devices. Each device are responsible for a small task, which lead to the redundancy and may harm to our health. The integration of multi-device is also needed.

Tasks:

Task1 In-mind friend

Your personal assistant Vivy can communication with you without any outer methods. Thanks to the brain-computer interface, there’s no need to interact with Vivy with voice or image, but mind! All the communication are all in your mind directly at any time. If on special occasions, the inner voice could also be shut down.

Task2 In-mind friend

Employing the most advanced Natural Language Processing(NLP) model, Vivy could communicate with you without any obstacles. With emotion analyzing model, Vivy could feel you emotion and give you the right feed back. She would laugh out with you when you are in good emotion and also comfort you when you are down. You could say every thing to her and never worried about your secret been spread out or this friend may laugh at you.

Task3 Individuated personality

Through Individuated personality, Vivy could interactive with you in different style. As a virtual human in you mind, Vivy could change her personality as you wish. A more outgoing personality or a introvert one are both optional. Moreover, according to your response, Vivy could also adjust attributes automatically.

Task4 Multi-dimensional interaction

Modern people have been overwhelmed by various electronic device. Not only their function are limited, but also they are hard to interact with. Vivy could solve the problem through combine all the functions of multiple devices. It could be you in-brain music player, watch, health detector and so on. You don’t have to staring at screen on your phone with your head down anymore. Vivy can do far more than just a smartphone.

Task5 Sense Enhancement

Human sense are always limited. When concentrating on something, it is possibly that one could make a mistake. Vivy can make you sense more sensitively by capturing all the environment change and note you what you have ignored. She is able to warn you before you crash your car, or point out you calculation mistake. With sense enhancement, life could be more easy and safe.
